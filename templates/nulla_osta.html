Nulla Osta Appointments

Expert guidance and step-by-step support for every type of Nulla Osta — Seasonal, Domestic, and Family Reunion.

A Nulla Osta is an official clearance required to work, study, or reunite with family in Italy. It ensures that the Italian authorities have approved your eligibility for the requested purpose.

Depending on your goals, there are different types of Nulla Osta: Seasonal for temporary work, Domestic for caregivers and household staff, and Family Reunion for joining loved ones legally residing in Italy.

At Tejas Overseas, we provide professional guidance for each type. Our consultants ensure that your documents are accurate, appointments are booked correctly, and the process is smooth and stress-free. We handle all the details so you can focus on your move to Italy with confidence.
