Family Reunion Nulla Osta Appointment

Expert guidance for reunification of families in Italy with professionalism and care.

The Family Reunion Nulla Osta is required for foreign nationals wishing to reunite with their family members legally residing in Italy. Tejas Overseas provides a complete consultancy solution — from document review, eligibility verification, and online submissions to embassy appointments and compliance checks. Our structured approach ensures the process is efficient, transparent, and stress-free.
