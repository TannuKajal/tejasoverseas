Seasonal Nulla Osta Appointment

Professional guidance for temporary work authorizations abroad — precise, reliable, and structured.

The Seasonal Nulla Osta is a mandatory government authorization for foreign nationals seeking temporary employment in regulated sectors such as agriculture, hospitality, and tourism. At Tejas Overseas, we provide end-to-end consultancy services, ensuring that every step of your application is executed with precision and adherence to official guidelines. From verifying eligibility and validating documentation to online registration and embassy coordination, our structured approach guarantees clarity, efficiency, and compliance.

Our Specialized Services
Comprehensive Document Verification

Our experts meticulously examine all relevant documentation — employment contracts, identification proofs, and supporting letters — ensuring compliance with governmental standards. This minimizes the risk of rejection and ensures your application is complete and accurate.

Digital Application Management

We guide you through the official online pre-registration portals, providing step-by-step assistance with form completion, document submission, and procedural compliance. Our attention to detail ensures that your digital application is flawless and adheres to official standards.

Priority Appointment Scheduling

We coordinate with relevant authorities to secure appointments efficiently. You receive timely notifications and guidance for every step, reducing delays and ensuring compliance with official schedules.

End-to-End Visa Preparation

After Nulla Osta approval, we assist with all subsequent visa procedures, including document submission at embassies, compliance checks, and interview preparation. Our structured methodology ensures that your transition from approval to employment is smooth and stress-free.
