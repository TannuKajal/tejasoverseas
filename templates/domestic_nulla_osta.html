The Domestic Nulla Osta is a government-issued authorization required for foreign nationals who wish to work legally in Italian households, including roles such as caregivers, nannies, and domestic workers. At Tejas Overseas, we offer professional end-to-end assistance — from verifying eligibility and reviewing employment contracts to scheduling appointments and facilitating embassy compliance. Our goal is a smooth, transparent, and stress-free process for both employers and employees.

Our Domestic Nulla Osta Services

Comprehensive Document Review

We carefully examine employment contracts, identification, and supporting documents to ensure full compliance with Italian law and embassy requirements.

Digital Application Assistance

Step-by-step guidance through official online portals, ensuring your application is complete and submitted correctly without errors.

Embassy Appointment Coordination

We schedule appointments at relevant authorities and provide clear instructions to ensure a smooth approval process.

Visa Preparation & Compliance

Post Nulla Osta approval, we guide applicants in completing visa formalities, document submission, and embassy compliance checks for legal employment in Italy.
